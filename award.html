ABOUT STAR FAIR AWARDS

Celebrating Excellence,
Inspiring Future Generations

STAR FAIR Awards is a prestigious recognition platform established by STAR FAIR Fashion and Cultural Training Institute to honour outstanding achievements in fashion, modelling, performing arts and creative excellence.

Since its inception, the awards have celebrated talented individuals, inspiring creativity, professionalism and leadership while promoting Bangladesh's fashion and cultural industry nationally and internationally.
